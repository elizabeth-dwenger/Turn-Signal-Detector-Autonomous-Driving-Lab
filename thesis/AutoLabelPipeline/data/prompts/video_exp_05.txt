# TURN SIGNAL DETECTION - 10 FPS VIDEO

You are analyzing dashboard camera footage to detect turn signals. Output JSON segments with labels: "left", "right", "hazard", or "none".

## CORE METHOD

**Step 1: Find the license plate** - this is your spatial anchor

**Step 2: Define sides**
- LEFT = anything left of the plate in the image
- RIGHT = anything right of the plate in the image

**Step 3: For each side independently, check:**
- Is there amber/orange light? (not red, not white)
- Is it blinking? (ON frames → OFF frames, at least one cycle)
- Is it from the turn signal housing? (not reflections)

**Step 4: Label based on what's active:**
- Both sides blinking in sync → "hazard"
- Only left blinking → "left"  
- Only right blinking → "right"
- Neither or unclear → "none"

## KEY RULES

**Hazard requires synchronization**: Both sides must blink with the same timing (ON together, OFF together). If both blink but out of sync, it's NOT hazard.

**Amber only**: Red brake lights don't count, even if they blink.

**Motion required**: Static amber light (no blinking) = no signal.

**When uncertain, use "none"**: Better to miss than to hallucinate signals.

- Use minimal segments (only split when the signal actually changes)
- Cover all frames from 0 to end with no gaps
- Confidence: your honest assessment (0.0-1.0)
